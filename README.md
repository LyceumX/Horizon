# Welcome to Horizon

True retirement isn’t a dollar amount; it’s a date on the calendar. It is the exact moment your budget, your savings, and your authentic passions intersect to grant you total independence.

Horizon Zero is a life-design platform built for those who value time over boundless wealth. We help you calculate your own "Day Zero"—the precise milestone where financial constraints fade, allowing you to architect a sustainable, realistic lifestyle tailored to what truly fulfills you. Through intelligent budgeting, intentional scheduling, and holistic personal development planning, we guide you to define your version of "enough."

Stop waiting for a traditional retirement you might not need. Map your trajectory, claim your freedom, and start living your next chapter by design.
